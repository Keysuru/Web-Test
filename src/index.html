Soundscapes

In crafting this poster for Yuronono's Toronto tour, I drew inspiration from the unique essence of Shoegaze music. This genre is characterized by its heavy locomotive-scrollortion and ethereal melodies, which I aimed to reflect visually. I began with classic analogue photography as the grain, noise and lighting would provide an intimate base. The result is a design that mirrors the raw and emotive nature of Shoegaze, creating a captivating and nostalgic experience for viewers.

Dead Lang

Inspired by the rich history of Nordic culture and the Vikings' maritime heritage, 'Dead Lang' Mead came to life as a fusion of Viking vitality and timeless skeletal aesthetics. The logo, uniting a Viking half-face and skeletal visage, embodies the essence of 'Dead Lang.' Embracing ancient Norse design, I shaped typography and glyphs to evoke an authentic Nordic ambiance. The flavor, a harmonious blend of salty brine and honey, celebrated the Vikings' seafaring spirit and guided the overall design. To honor Raven Brewing, a subtle nod to their name seamlessly integrated with the label. 'Dead Lang' represents the synergy of life and death, where artistry and storytelling harmonize, all influenced by the mead's unique character.

Z Journe-E

This captivating T-shirt design is a visual voyage through the realm of collage aesthetics. It artfully blends elements of the past with the present, capturing the essence of nostalgia film-wave of the 90s, a phenomenon that continues to captivate a generation. It evokes the feelings of rediscovery and wonderment, offering a window into the past while remaining firmly rooted in the contemporary.
